About The Application

This platform has been developed by using the Victor Terrón FungiRegEx software. This platform will help you to:

  • Generate transcriptions.
  • Generate translations.
  • Generate phylogenetic trees.
  • Check Regular Expressions in Proteome of Fungi.
  • Check Regular Expressions in Proteome of Algues.
A platform designed to assist in the implementation of the ISO/IEC 29110 standard can provide specialized resources, management and monitoring tools, as well as expert support to help organizations implement the standard effectively and achieve significant improvements in their engineering practices of software and systems. This web app makes the process of transcribing DNA sequences into complementary RNA sequences a breeze. Whether you're working with genetic data for gene expression analysis or other molecular biology studies, our transcription generator ensures accurate and efficient results.

Easily translate mRNA sequences into the corresponding amino acid sequences with BioTranscribe's translation feature. This function is invaluable for deciphering the genetic code and understanding the proteins encoded by your genes of interest. Dive into the fascinating world of evolutionary biology and genetics with BioTranscribe's phylogenetic tree builder. Construct phylogenetic trees to visualize the evolutionary relationships among species or genetic sequences, aiding in evolutionary and comparative genomics research. For researchers studying fungal proteomes, BioTranscribe provides a specialized tool to validate and analyze regular expressions. Ensure the accuracy of your pattern matching and data analysis in fungal proteomic studies. Extend your proteomic analysis capabilities to algae with BioTranscribe's dedicated proteome regular expression checker. This feature offers precision and efficiency for researchers working with algae proteomes, streamlining data analysis.
